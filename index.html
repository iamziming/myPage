About

Hi everyone, I'm a new graduate from George Brown College, I achieved overall GPA 3.8 at school. I have always been passionate about web programming. My most recent work experience was an IT helper at George Brown College during the last semester, which mainly included setting up computers, routers and switches in the labs. Also, software installation, activation and registration. I believe I am a quick and eager learner, whenever theres something interests me I would google a bunch of videos and start watching right away.

I am committed to meet deadlines. During my last job I was urged to set up 3 labs in one week, each lab had about 50 computers and I was only doing it after class in my spare time. The deadline was really tight, rather than go home and ignore the problem, I stayed late and finished the task on time.

I am a problem solver and I like to take a problem and look at it from different angle. I also enjoy doing work that challenges me and pushes me to think outside the box.
